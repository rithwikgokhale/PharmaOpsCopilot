# SOP: Bioreactor Operations

## SOP-BIO-OPS-001 — Operating Ranges

Fermentation temperature target 37.0°C (acceptable 36.5–37.5°C). pH target 7.10 (acceptable 6.95–7.25). Agitation target 180 rpm (acceptable 170–190 rpm).

## SOP-BIO-OPS-002 — pH Control

If pH trends below lower limit, operator may add buffer per approved addition chart. Document volume, lot number, and timestamp in batch record.

## SOP-BIO-OPS-003 — Temperature Excursions

Temperature above 37.5°C for more than 15 minutes requires deviation initiation and QA notification.

## SOP-BIO-OPS-004 — Agitator Monitoring

Agitator speed below 170 rpm during fermentation must be investigated. Check mechanical coupling and drive motor current.
