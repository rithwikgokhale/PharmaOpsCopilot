# SOP: CIP Cleaning Procedure

## SOP-CIP-001 — Purpose

Defines clean-in-place procedures for bioreactor trains including conductivity rinse verification.

## SOP-CIP-002 — Conductivity Acceptance

Final rinse conductivity must be below 0.10 mS/cm before batch release from CIP. Extended holds are permitted but must be documented with start/end timestamps.

## SOP-CIP-003 — Hold Conditions

If conductivity threshold is not met within expected window, hold the CIP cycle and notify utilities. Do not proceed to batch start until acceptance criteria are met.

## SOP-CIP-004 — Sensor Verification

CIP conductivity sensor response time should be verified per PM schedule WO-752. Stale sensor data may cause extended rinse cycles.
